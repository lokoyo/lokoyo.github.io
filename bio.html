Biography of Pong-Ravee "Win" Halelamien

Born and raised in Miami, Florida, Win started as a shy Thai guy. As his adventure through childhood progressed, he gradually became more quirky and charismatic. At his high school, Ransom Everglades, he was active in his high school's BattleBots team. In his 4 years of high school, he had only missed 5 days of robotics which was due to illness. He held the position of Quartermaster and President. By his senior year, he was inducted in the Chinese Honor Society, National Honor Society, and Mu Alpha Theta. In additon to these honors, Win has interned at several various companies and organizations such as Double Robotics and the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration. He recieved the fortunate opportunity to attend Tufts University where he is majoring in Mechanical Engineering and minoring in Human Factors and Entrepeneur Leadership. When he is not busy thinking up another project or product, he enjoys juggling, spinning poi, and practicing parkour. His future goals include being the juggling fire, creating his own startup company, and being the best human being he could possibly be. Fun fact: Win has a collection of over 60 different design of Pocky boxes.
